1. Introduction

The transportation of alternative fuels represents a critical challenge in the global transition to sustainable energy systems. As hydrogen, methanol, and ammonia emerge as key carriers for clean energy, the complexity of calculating accurate transportation costs has grown exponentially. Each fuel presents unique handling requirements, safety protocols, and economic considerations that traditional logistics models fail to address comprehensively.

FuelRoute Pro addresses this challenge through an AI-powered transportation cost calculator specifically designed for alternative fuels. The system integrates OpenAI's GPT-4 model with real-world geographic data, industry-standard transport parameters, and fuel-specific handling requirements to provide accurate, actionable cost estimates for fuel logistics professionals.

The platform supports three primary alternative fuels: liquid hydrogen (LH‚ÇÇ) requiring cryogenic storage at -253¬∞C, ammonia (NH‚ÇÉ) necessitating specialized safety protocols for toxic gas handling, and methanol (CH‚ÇÉOH) offering liquid-state transport advantages but requiring chemical handling procedures. Unlike conventional fuel transport calculators, FuelRoute Pro accounts for the full spectrum of costs including specialized equipment, safety compliance, insurance premiums, and regulatory requirements specific to each fuel type.
